How It Works

Introducing EvacSafely Advisor

An AI-powered "Mission Control" designed to close the gap between having data and taking action—globally—by fusing geospatial precision with the reasoning power of advanced Large Language Models.

The "Where"

Geospatial Core

Powered by a highly complex, real-time data pipeline leveraging the best of Google and Esri technologies, we ingest massive streams of global crisis feeds simultaneously. Our spatial engine instantly calculates the exact proximity of a disaster polygon to critical infrastructure and populations — even when the source data is unstructured.

The "What Now?"

Unstructured Data Analysis

Our secret weapon. EvacSafely can read unstructured data — dense PDF reports, raw field notes — and instantly extract the exact intelligence a commander needs. It transforms chaos into structured strategies aligned with UNDRR, EU Civil Protection, MENA regional protocols, and U.S. national emergency response frameworks.

The "How"

Automated Situation Reporting

Documentation is the backbone of recovery. Our platform automates professional Situation Reports (SITREPs) and Evacuation Plans — synthesizing weather forecasts, demographics, and AI-driven advice into a downloadable PDF in seconds.

Platform Capabilities

All the Tools You Need to Succeed

Our platform seamlessly integrates AI and geospatial technologies for real-time data, intelligent simulations, and advanced analytics.

Evacuation

Intelligent Evacuation Zones

Automatically generates evacuation zones based on risk assessments, population density, and transportation infrastructure for targeted planning and resource optimization.

Traffic

Intelligent Traffic Control

Manages traffic flow, optimizes routes, and implements dynamic traffic control measures to ensure efficient movement of evacuees during emergencies.

Flood

Flash Flood Simulation

Predicts the spread of floods and identifies at-risk areas, assisting in determining evacuation zones and prioritizing efforts in flood-prone regions.

Wildfire

Wildfire Modeling Tools

Predicts the spread of wildfires and identifies high-risk areas, aiding in developing evacuation strategies and coordinating response efforts.

A True Multilingual Lifeline

Disasters don't speak one language. Whether it's generating an executive summary in Arabic for a wildfire in Central Africa or an evacuation plan in English for flooding in Louisiana, EvacSafely ensures that local authorities and citizens get critical intelligence in their native language — when seconds count.

The Vision

Our mission is to democratize high-end Emergency Operations Center (EOC) capabilities. A local municipality shouldn't need a massive enterprise budget to access the same geospatial and AI tools as a global relief agency.

We are moving emergency management from asking "What happened?" to knowing exactly "What do we do next?"
